# NON_GOALS

This product is intentionally not:

1. A warehouse management system (WMS).
2. A spreadsheet replacement for every operational use case.
3. A prescriptive coaching/people-management system.
4. A full ETL and data-cleansing platform.

## Why

The product focus is operational signal interpretation, trust transparency, and follow-through support. Scope is constrained to keep the daily workflow clear and usable for supervisors.
